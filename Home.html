Vanguard

is the district's state-of-the-art innovation facility for students in grades 9-12. With a number of labs, a makerspace, a workshop, a build shop, and a vocational agriculture center. The Vanguard Academy is ideal for students who crave an active, project-based learning environment.

XPO

is a full-time, project-based learning program for students in grades 9-12. Innovators earn core credits through interdisciplinary courses and elective credits through a multitude of experiences focused on exposing, expanding, and exploring various subjects. Innovators will take a math and science class called Scimatics and an English and history class called Connect. Innovators will spend their senior year working on a human-centered design project that tackles their passions and/or community problems.

Broken Arrow Design Lab

is a full-time, Human Centered Design program for students in grades 9-12. Innovators earn core and elective credits through a combination of immersion, seminar, and design experiences while incorporating human experiences and knowledge. Innovators will spend their senior year working on a human-centered design project that tackles their passions and/or community problems.
