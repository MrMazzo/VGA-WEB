XPO

Core courses descriptions

Connect

XPO allows innovators to participate in Project Based Learning (PBL). Connect is the name given to a co-taught History and English course. This Connect course focuses on connecting historical figures and events to our current evolving world while utilizing skills developed through English Language Arts. Community-focused innovators work together on larger projects to develop an authentic experience. Connect will give innovators a voice and choice in their project path, while also allowing them to discover multiple professional avenues available to them after high school.

SCIMATICS

A fusion of Math and Science subjects working alongside CONNECT courses to achieve integration between subjects through projects intended to engage and support the Broken Arrow community. In this course, student engagement is encouraged through the application of the standards, allowing students to discover and experience their usefulness. Students' knowledge and growth will be assessed using traditional methods as well as throughout the Project Based Learning process.

E.E.I

The XPO program offers a dynamic, project-based learning experience for grades 9, 10 and 11, fostering innovation and problem-solving skills crucial for future success. This dynamic experience is called E.E.I or intro to XPO , Explore and Expose will be core classes that Vanguard innovators will have through their high school carer some of the key features of this classes are XPO Explore: Project-based learning for grade 11 students, with a focus on self-directed interdisciplinary projects to expose real-world issues, Expose: Project-based learning for grade 10 students, with a focus on different professions and nine focus areas of study , Intro To XPO: Project-based course introducing essential skills, covering software, tool usage, 3D printing, woodworking, and more.
