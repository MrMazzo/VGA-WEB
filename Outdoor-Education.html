Outdoor Education

Outdoor E.D Elective

In this course, students from the main High School and Vanguard engage in a variety of outdoor recreational activities to develop a love and appreciation for outdoor experiences.

Outward Bound

Embark on a journey of self-discovery in our Outward Bound course, where outdoor expeditions and leadership challenges await with a overview in environmental Science.Challenge yourself with thrilling activities like rock climbing and wilderness navigation. Develop leadership skills through team-building exercises and off-campus expeditions.Our adventure-based approach inspires growth and confidence, guiding students through experiences that foster teamwork and problem-solving.Our assessments focus on personal growth and leadership development, including peer evaluations and self-reflections. Through feedback and reflection, students emerge with newfound confidence and resilience.

Primitive skills

Discover essential survival techniques and deepen your connection to nature in our Primitive Skills course.Master primitive skills, navigation, and wilderness first aid while engaging in hands-on activities like fire-making and shelter-building.Immerse in experiential learning with a focus on practical skills and self-reliance. Develop resilience through group activities and reflective discussions.Assessments include tests, projects, quizzes, and practical demonstrations, fostering personal growth and accountability.
